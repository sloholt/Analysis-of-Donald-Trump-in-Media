# Codebook: Trump Media Coverage Analysis (Draft)

**Coder:** [Your Name]
**Date:** [Current Date]

## Instructions
1. Read the article title and description (and full text if necessary).
2. Assign a **Primary Topic** from the list below.
3. (Optional) Assign a **Secondary Topic** if the article clearly covers two distinct themes.
4. Assign a **Sentiment** score.

---

## 1. Topic Categories (Draft)
*These categories will be refined after the initial open coding of 50-100 articles.*

| Code | Topic Name | Description / Keywords |
|------|------------|------------------------|
| **LEGAL** | Legal Investigations & Trials | Indictments, lawsuits, court appearances, fraud trials, hush money, E. Jean Carroll. |
| **ELECTION** | Elections & Campaigning | 2016/2020/2024 campaigns, rallies, polls, debates, primaries, VP picks. |
| **JAN6** | January 6th & Democracy | Capitol riot, election denial, "Stop the Steal", democracy threats. |
| **POLICY** | Policy & Governance | Immigration (border wall), trade (tariffs), taxes, healthcare, foreign policy decisions. |
| **PERSONAL** | Personal Life & Business | Family (Melania, Ivanka), golf, Trump Organization business, health, wealth. |
| **MEDIA** | Media & Culture | Feuds with press, Truth Social, celebrity feuds, pop culture references. |
| **GOP** | Republican Party Politics | Relationship with GOP, endorsements, infighting, McCarthy/McConnell. |
| **OTHER** | Other | Anything not fitting the above categories. |

---

## 2. Sentiment Coding

| Code | Label | Criteria |
|------|-------|----------|
| **POS** | Positive | Portrays Trump in a favorable light; focuses on achievements, praise, or exoneration. |
| **NEG** | Negative | Portrays Trump unfavorably; focuses on scandals, failures, criticism, or legal trouble. |
| **NEU** | Neutral | Factual reporting without loaded language; balanced "he said/she said" coverage. |
